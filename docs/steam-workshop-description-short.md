[h1]History & Rankings[/h1]

[b]For Civilization VII[/b]
A post-game archive and leaderboard. Every finished game is saved to a persistent archive you can revisit from the main menu: a ranked board of every civilization, a turn-by-turn replay of the map you played on, each leader's lineage across the ages, and a chronicle of the game's defining moments. The goal is to make a finished game readable as a lived history.

[b]The Mod Includes:[/b]
[list]
[*]Civilization Rankings: a gold/silver/bronze podium and a ranked list of every run by its in-game score, headlined by Leader and lineage, with civ-progression chips and each run's final result.
[*]World Leader titles: a Civ V-style honor roll — your best game is crowned Cincinnatus, with historical leaders down the ladder for the rest.
[*]Historical Map: a Civ V-style hex-minimap replay of the actual game world — water, land, and mountains, each civ's territory in its real color, cities marked, a turn scrubber, and a legend.
[*]Lineage: each leader's civilization across every age.
[*]Chronicle: a fully local, template-built narrative of the game.
[*]Click any ranked run to open its Lineage, Chronicle, and Historical Map.
[/list]

[b]What The Mod Does Not Do:[/b]
[list]
[*]Gameplay balance is left untouched.
[*]Rival AI behaves exactly as it would without the mod.
[*]No base-game files are replaced or overwritten.
[/list]

[b]Compatibility:[/b]
[list]
[*]Read-only presentation layer.
[*]No base-game file replacement.
[*]Per-save data storage; the archive survives restarts.
[*]Renders cleanly at any resolution, from 1366x768 up to 4K.
[/list]

[b]Installation:[/b]
[list=1]
[*]Download or subscribe to the mod.
[*]Place the [b]history-and-rankings[/b] folder in the Civilization VII Mods directory.
[*]Enable History & Rankings from Additional Content in-game.
[/list]

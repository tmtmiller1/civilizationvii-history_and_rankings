[h1]History & Rankings[/h1]

A post-game archive and leaderboard for Civilization VII. The top 25 finished games are saved to a persistent archive you can revisit from the main menu, including in progress games when applicable. 

History & Rankings turns each game into a record you can study: a ranked board of every lead-civilization combo, a reduce mini-map style turn-by-turn replay of the world map you actually played on, each leader's lineage across the ages, and a chronicle of the game's defining moments. Storytelling is the core goal by making past games readable as a lived history rather than a screen you click past and never see again.

[b]Note:[/b]
This mod is a work in progress as I simply cannot test all scenarios and resolutions and computer types and configurations. If you see an issue, or find something annoying, PLEASE let me know! Your feedback is the only way I can improve it.


[b]The Mod Includes:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Civilization Rankings[/b] — your past games as a leaderboard. A gold/silver/bronze top-three podium sits beside a ranked list of every run by its [b]in-game score[/b] (the civilization's own score, the same value the game keeps), each entry headlined by Leader and civilization lineage (for example "Augustus · Rome → America"), with colored civ-progression chips and the run's final result (the age it reached, or the turn it was eliminated).
[*][b]World Leader titles[/b] — in the spirit of Civilization V's end-game honor roll, every run earns a historical world-leader title based on how it scores against your best game. Reach the top of the board and you are crowned [b]Cincinnatus[/b]; lesser runs fall down the ladder toward the bottom.
[*][b]Historical Map[/b] — a Civilization V-style replay of the actual game world. A downsampled hex minimap shows water, land, and mountains with each civilization's territory filled in its real color, cities marked, a turn scrubber to play the empires' expansion through the whole game, and a legend mapping colors to civilizations.
[*][b]Lineage[/b] — follow each leader's civilization across every age of the game.
[*][b]Chronicle[/b] — a fully local, template-built narrative of how the game unfolded.
[*]Click any ranked run to open its Lineage, Chronicle, and Historical Map.
[/list]

[b]Details:[/b]
[list]
[*]Cross-session persistence: your games archive and per-run map replays survive restarts, and the main menu lists your top games.
[*]Renders cleanly at any resolution, from 1366x768 up to 4K.
[*]Localized in eleven languages: English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Brazilian Portuguese, Russian, Simplified Chinese, and Polish.
[*]Readable, un-minified source.
[/list]

[b]Notes:[/b] Read-only and additive: it does not change balance, alter opponents, or overwrite base-game files. Per-save data storage. Install: place the [b]history-and-rankings[/b] folder in the Civ VII Mods folder and enable it from Additional Content.
